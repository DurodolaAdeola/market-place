ABOUT US

Foodlocker offers procurement efficiency, affordability, convenience, and pricing regularity to diverse buyers of farm-fresh foodstuff and grocery items by aggregating the outputs of smallholder farmers and fast-moving consumer goods companies and selling them directly to our customers via our unique omni-channel retail operations. Our vision is to become the one-stop shop for farm-fresh foodstuff and grocery items in Africa that addresses the logistical and infrastructural gaps in the food value chain across Africa. Many people will tell you "Retail is Detail". At Foodlocker, we believe "Less is More". Our aim is not to become the largest retail store in Nigeria with the broadest selection of products. We started Foodlocker to provide the right selection of top quality foodstuff to you through our no-frills, fairly priced, locally-sourced, and unbeatably convenient shopping model.

WHO IS A FOODLOCKER

  1. A Foodlocker understands that the margin between price and value has to be very thin. The price you pay should be as close to the value you get as possible.
  2. A Foodlocker is right a lot. Even if he/she makes a mistake, they own up and fix it quickly.
  3. A Foodlocker has integrity. You can trust him/her.
  4. A Foodlocker understands basic numbers. They can easily estimate in their head.
  5. A Foodlocker loves farmers. He/she relishes the opportunity to spend time on the farm, to understand the products, to learn from the farmers, and to respect the effort that went into the cultivation/rearing process.
  6. A Foodlocker rolls up his/her sleeves to get work done. No task is beneath a real Foodlocker. You will NEVER hear him/her say "that is not my job". A Foodlocker is NEVER too posh to help.
